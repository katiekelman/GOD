About Us!

As a charity we work closely with the National Activity Providers Association. They are a great organisation that helps care teams with training, ideas and help. Their aim is to “assist every care and support setting to be full of life, love and laughter”.
We sponsor an annual award to the resident of a care facility who have been helped and encouraged by their carers to do something “outrageous or with a fabulous twist that defies expectations.” The achievements in the past winners have been truly amazing and produced some wonderful role models for elderly people
This year, in addition to that award, we are organising an art competition for the over 90s. The winner will receive the Grow Old Disgracefully Art Prize which will consist of £500 and a tree of their choice to be planted near their home. Details of how to enter for this competition will be posted on this site in the next few weeks.
