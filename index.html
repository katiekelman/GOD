Improve the quality of life for the elderly

As a charity we work closely with the National Activity Providers Association. They are a great organisation that helps care and support settings to prioritise wellbeing, by promoting activities, arts and engagement. Their aim is to “assist every setting to be full of life, love and laughter”.

We sponsor an annual award organised by NAPA, to the resident of a care facility who has been helped and encouraged by their carers to do something “outrageous or with a fabulous twist that defies expectations.” The achievements of the past winners have been truly amazing and produced some wonderful role models for elderly people.
