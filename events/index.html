Dame Judi Dench

It was a wonderful collection of paintings and picking a winner was not an easy task! However, after much deliberation I thought the one by Felicity Gladstone was the best. I loved the humour in it, the two ladies enjoying a cup of tea in the garden with the mobility scooter parked on the path and the Zimmer frame in the corner. A lovely painting capturing the essence of the times we are now living in!
