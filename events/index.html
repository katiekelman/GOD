The grow old disgracefully art prize for artists over 90

Entries are now closed. A BIG thank you to all of you who have taken part and everyone who has encouraged and helped them to do so. The quality and number of entries has overwhelmed me and it has given us enormous pleasure each day seeing what has arrived by email. Some of the paintings have shown a skill that would be acceptable in any open art competition. Many have made us laugh. Some have made us speechless. A lot of them tell a wonderful story and most have been done by people determined not to put off by age, illness or disability.

Sally Bulgin
