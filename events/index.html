The grow old disgracefully art prize for artists over 90

In this “year of the lockdown” we are giving a prize for the best artwork entitled “Happy Times” produced by an artist over the age of 90 at the end of August this year. The pictures must be 2D (flat), but can be in any media – pencil, charcoal, crayon, watercolour, acrylic, oils, collage, digital – or a mixture of the whole lot if you are feeling especially disgraceful!

Email a photo of your artwork to competitions@growolddisgracefully.co.uk with the subject "Happy Times" along with your name, date of birth, size of the piece and the media you use.

Adjudication

The best entries will be shortlisted and displayed on our website for the duration of September 2020. A winner will be picked by our judges and announced at the end of the same month.

The Rules

    • The competition is open to all UK citizens who are over 90 on 31st August 2020
    • Each person may only submit one work of art that has been created in 2020.
    • We must receive the entries for the competition by 31 August 2020. We will let you know by the middle of September if your picture has been shortlisted.
    • The final winner will be announced by the end of September and will receive £200 and a tree of their choice to be planted near their home.
    • When taking a photo of your artwork to send to us, please make sure you use natural lighting. Dark or unnatural light can result in odd-looking images.
    • We will supply a young sapling and, with the agreement of the winner, arrange for it to be planted in a suitable place. It will be up to the winner, together with the owner of the site, to decide what sort of tree they would like.
    • The Grow Old Disgracefully Trust reserves the right to cancel the competition at any time, if deemed necessary or if circumstances arise outside of our control.
    • The competition will be entirely online. You do not need to send us the original.
